               Artists &amp; Fleas
              </a>
              \n
             </h3>
             \n            \n
             <div class="page_meta_controls">

             <p class="feature_item__annotation--truncated">
              \n                        We Angelenos welcome New York transplants with open arms\u2014as long as they involve either gourmet ice cream\xa0or artisanal, handcrafted goodies. These criteria make the Arts District debut of Brooklyn-born craft market\xa0Artists &amp; Fleas a no-brainer. Every third weekend of the month, stroll along Mateo St (between 6th and 7th Sts) from 11am to 5pm, for an outdoor weekend event, featuring locally made art, fashion, vintage wares, vinyl, edibles and beyond. Mingle amongst LA\'s hippest while eyeing hand-beaded jewelry or one-of-a-kind reclaimed wood work from 75+ vendors. Plus, there\'s a "feed lot" each weekend that features five LA-based food trucks; past participants include Coolhaus, Currywurst and Komodo. Admission is free. Want to get in on the vending action? LA artisans, designers, vintage collectors and food truckers can contact Artists &amp; Fleas\xa0here\xa0for more info on how to participate.\xa0\n

             <p class="feature_item__annotation--truncated">
              \n                        Enjoy Thursday evenings outdoors in Pasadena\'s Playhouse District for this charming night of food and music. Grab a glass of wine at Monopole Wine or a cup of coffee at Zona Rosa Caffe while you chow down on food from regularly returning trucks like Kogi, Grilled Cheese Truck, Hungry Nomad and Crepe\'n Around. You\'ll find extra spots to sit and a bar in the Pasadena Playhouse courtyard as well as live music (often featuring members of Ozomatli)\xa0in the alleyway next to Zona Rosa. Check the website for the full food truck and music schedule.\n
